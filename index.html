Element Duel

Game Rules

  • Fire is super effective against nature
  • Water is super effective against Fire
  • Nature is super effective against Water
  • Both player and computer roll a 20 sided die
  • If you pick an element that is effective against the computer's choice you get an advantage
  • If you and the computer pick the same element no advantages are given

How To Play

  1. Click the Begin Duel button
  2. Select your element and the dice will be rolled
